FAQS

The Following are Question which we recieve most often:

Are all the pets which you rescue up for adoption?
Yes and no, since before we put the rescued pets up for adoption we must first ensure that the animals are ready phsycially and mentally to be around humans first. After that we must also ensure the individual(s) who is looking to adopt is a good fit for our pets for the safety of the animals so they don't go through what they did previously.
What does it cost to adopt a pet from the shelter?
They generally range from $25 to $250. Sometimes the pets are if there is a special promotion we have going on, usuallly during Holiday Time.
How long have you guys been around for?
We are a generally new Pet Rescue Shelter as we just opened our facility and cause out to the public on May 9th, 2023. However, we have been rescuing pets and been providing for them for a few months now.
How long does the adoption porcess take?
The adoption process can range from a mere few minutes to a few days. It all depends on the various policies which we have for specific pets as well as how well we feel the connection the people are adopting have with the animal.
If I have found a pet I'm interesting in. What do I do next?
First off, Congragulations! The next steps which you would have to take is head over to our Contact Page where you could fill out a simple form at the bottom of the page.
