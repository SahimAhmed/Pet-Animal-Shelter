The two images seen on the left are a before and after of our very first cat at the facility called Miro. He is of a Ragdoll breed which are distinct for their colorprint coat and their bright blue eyes. The image on the left is when we first found him and the image on the left is around the time he turned 1 years old. Miro has a very bi-polar personality as he sometimes shows his extreme energy or his extreme laziness, but he is fantastic regardless. He was born on March 25, 2022.

This is Ruby, an 11 year old Golden Retriever, a breed known for their undeniable loyalty and affection. Ruby is incredibly friendly, generous, and gentle. Unfortunetly, the older a pet is, the less likely they are to be adopted. However we are happy to say that Ruby was rescued by a family in Connecticut, eager to welcome this joyous dog into their home.

To the left are Chikies and Milo. These two brother and sister Shih-tzus puppies are 6 months old and are looking for a good home. Adopting this pair is a great option to allow them to burn off that crazy puppy energy. Shih Tzus are a social breed, not only do they love to be around humans, but they enjoy the company of other dogs too. To the right is Frog. He is two years old and was found in the alley. Although he's socially awkward he will grace you with a heartwarming headbutt that will make you feel special.

Meet Kemba and Kali, Miniature Poodle siblings, a breed renowned for their intelligence and lively personalities. Despite being siblings, they each have their own quirks - Kemba is the more adventurous of the two, while Kali loves to snuggle up for a cuddle. Often, potential adopters may overlook bonded pairs, fearing the additional responsibility. However, we are thrilled to share that these spirited siblings found their forever home with a loving family in New Jersey, who are eager to embrace the double dose of joy and companionship.
