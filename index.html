Welcome to our Pet Rescue Shelter. Our goal and objective is to help pets of all kinds and species who have been abandoned, treated poorly, and in need of a new home. We are a 100% non-profit organization that soley want to help lost pets or anyone's pets in our community.

Our staff members are highly trained and dedicated to such cause as they also have had pets which they had rescued and provided a home for as well. If you or anyone you know have found a pet lost in the streets looking for food or any of the other previous stated situations please contact us Now!
