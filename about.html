Saving One Pet at a Time

Our Pet Rescue Shelter is a non-profit organization devoted to the care and love of our furry friends. Our team works tirelessly to nurture abandon pets; we provide them with attentive vet care, quality food, and calming environments. However, our pets need a home.

We offer meet and greets with our pets, through which you will receieve information about each animal to find which one is the perfect fit for you. Each one of our animals are unique, but they all have lots of cuteness and love to give! Our goal is to help you find a pet who will fill your life with love and companionship.

Why We Care

Only 10% of dogs born find a permanent home

There are roughly 30-40 stray and uncared for cats in the United States

At the Pet Rescue Shelter Center, we seek to educate pet owners on the dangers of abadnonment and mistreatment. We, as well as many other shelters in the nation, see more animals entering our center than leaving to a loving home. Many of our animals are also left to experience life long trauma and health problems. They deserve better than this.

Statistics from The Zebra, "Pet Adoption Statistics", 2023.
